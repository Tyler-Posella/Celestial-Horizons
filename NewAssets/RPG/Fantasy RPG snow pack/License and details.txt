FANTASY RPG - Snow expansion pack (by Franuka) v1.3
-------------------------
Thank you for purchasing this asset pack! This is an expansion to the original Fantasy RPG asset pack (https://franuka.itch.io/rpg-asset-pack)
It was created from scratch, and licensed under the Creative commons BY 4.0 (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)
Feel free to use it on any commercial or non-commercial game, just remember to add a link to my itch page and/or Twitter, it helps a lot :)
---
Included are all the tiles and spritesheets for each asset, in .png format, transparent background.
You should be able to recreate anything you see on the example images.
Everything comes in three sizes: 1x (16x16px tiles), 2x (32x32px tiles) and 3x (48x48px tiles).
